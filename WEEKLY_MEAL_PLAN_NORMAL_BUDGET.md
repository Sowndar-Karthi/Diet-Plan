## What This Plan Is Built For

| Goal | How This Plan Achieves It |
|---|---|
| **Live up to 100 years** | Anti-inflammatory foods, omega-3 fish, antioxidant fruits, whole grains — slow cell aging |
| **Heart health (lifelong)** | Soluble fiber grains, omega-3 fish, seeds, nuts — keeps arteries clean for decades |
| **Height & bone strength** | Ragi (highest calcium grain), fish, eggs, moringa, sesame — builds bones from young age |
| **Zero protein deficiency** | Every day covers 55–75g protein via fish + eggs + dal + legumes + millets combination |
| **Strong immunity** | Seasonal fruits, garlic, moringa, turmeric — activates body's natural defense |
| **Gut & digestive health** | Fermented foods (idli, dosa, adai), curd, buttermilk — healthy gut = healthy life |
| **Blood & energy** | Iron from ragi, greens, sesame; B12 from eggs and fish; zinc from pumpkin seeds |

## Daily Water Schedule (Same Every Day — All 7 Days)

| # | Time | How Much | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| 💧 **Glass 1** | **6:10 AM** | 1 full glass (250 ml) warm water | Flushes overnight toxins, activates metabolism |
| 💧 **Glass 2** | **11:00 AM** | 1 full glass before snack | Aids digestion, prevents overeating |
| 💧 **Glass 3** | **4:00 PM** | 1 full glass before evening snack | Controls hunger, supports kidney function |
| 💧 **Throughout day** | With meals & between | Sip regularly | Target **2–2.5 litres (8–10 glasses)** daily |

> **Bone & height tip:** Add a pinch of **rock salt (இந்துப்பு)** + squeeze of lemon to your 6:10 AM water. This alkalizes the body and helps calcium absorption for bone growth.
