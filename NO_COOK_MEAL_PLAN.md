# 7-Day No-Cook Meal Plan
> **Zero cooking required. Every item is soaked, sprouted, store-bought roasted, or raw-assembled.**
> **Designed specifically to lower LDL cholesterol from High level.**

---

## What This Plan Targets

| Mechanism | Foods Used in This Plan |
|---|---|
| Soluble fiber (blocks LDL absorption) | Oats, chia seeds, flaxseeds, sprouted legumes, apples, psyllium |
| Omega-3 fatty acids (reduces LDL) | Walnuts, flaxseeds, chia seeds |
| Plant sterols (competes with cholesterol) | Almonds, sunflower seeds, pumpkin seeds |
| Probiotics (gut-liver axis) | Low-fat curd, buttermilk |
| Antioxidants (prevents LDL oxidation) | Guava, papaya, seasonal fruits, raw salads |

---

## Daily Non-Negotiables (Add to Every Day)

- **6:00 AM** — 1 glass warm water + **1 tsp Isabgol (psyllium husk)** — single most powerful LDL reducer
- **Post-breakfast** — 1 cup **green tea** (no sugar) — lowers LDL by 5–7%
- **In any salad** — 1 raw garlic clove (crushed) + drizzle of **cold-pressed flaxseed oil or olive oil**
- **Dairy** — Use only **low-fat curd / skimmed milk** — full-fat raises LDL
- **Avoid** — Coconut oil, butter, ghee, full-fat paneer, fried snacks, biscuits, white bread

## Sprouting Guide (Prep Done Overnight — Not Cooking)

| Legume | Soak Time | Sprout Time | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Moong Dal | 8 hours | 12–24 hours | Easiest; rinse twice daily |
| Chana (chickpea) | 12 hours | 24–36 hours | Wrap in wet cloth after soaking |
| Masoor | 6 hours | 12 hours | Fastest sprouting |
| Mixed lentils | 8 hours | 12–24 hours | Rinse well |

> Sprout in bulk on Sunday and Wednesday. Store sprouted legumes in fridge for 3–4 days.

## Foods to Strictly Avoid

| Avoid | Why |
|---|---|
| Ghee, butter, coconut oil | High saturated fat — raises LDL directly |
| Full-fat milk, full-fat paneer | Saturated fat |
| Biscuits, namkeen, chips | Trans fats + refined carbs spike LDL |
| White bread, maida items | Refined carbs convert to triglycerides |
| Palm oil (many packaged snacks) | Saturated fat |
| Red meat, organ meats | Dietary cholesterol + saturated fat |
| Fried anything | Oxidized cholesterol |
| Coconut chutney/milk | Saturated fat |
